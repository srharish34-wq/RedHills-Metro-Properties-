Our Story

Built in Redhills,
trusted across Chennai

Redhills Metro Properties began in March 2009 from a single office at No 9, Krishna Complex, Vallavar Street, Redhills — with one simple focus: selling property, done right. Seventeen years on, that focus hasn't changed. We still only do one thing, and we've spent every one of those years getting better at it.

Today our dedicated sales team works seven days a week, 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM, guiding families, builders and NRI investors through residential plots, farm and agricultural land, commercial plots, warehouse land, and full house & villa construction — every single sale backed by CMDA, DTCP and RERA approvals.
